After completing a bachelors of applied science in electrical engineering from Queen's in 2004, he emigrated to Sydney, Australia to design cell phone base stations for the mobile carrier Optus. Finding the human element lacking in engineering, Greg Matthew returned to Canada in 2006 to pursue a bachelors of design in industrial design. Since graduating from OCADU in 2010, he has worked as a furniture designer, an invention consultant and a package and merchandising designer for a tool reseller in Toronto.

Greg Matthew is also currently pursuing his masters of design in Strategic Foresight and Innovation at OCADU.
